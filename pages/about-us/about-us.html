System's Purpose

The CTU Buddy was created with one intension in mind. This intension is focused mainly on helping you to better your academic experience.
To increase the experience for you, the CTU Buddy was devided into 6 catagories, namely: Home page, Timetable, Discussion, Share
Resources, About us and Contact us pages. Each page was made to help you in diffrent ways, from asking questions in discussions to
sharing pdf's in share recsources.

Vision

Our vision is to help learners reach their CTU needs by enhanching their academic experience. Through the use of our website and the people who use it.

Mission

The mission of CTU Buddy is to connect with you in an intimant setting. Where you feel comfortable enough to ask questions or to give suggestions. So that you can excel at your academics
